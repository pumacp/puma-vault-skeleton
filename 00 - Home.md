# 🏠 PUMA Vault Skeleton — Home

> A generic, reusable, self-documenting knowledge vault. It organizes complex, abstract, and fuzzy information into high-quality structured knowledge — and explains its own workings **using its own methods**.

## The method in one loop
This vault runs the **Capture → Organize → Distill → Express** cycle ([[CeReBro (CODE)]]):

| Phase | Method | Where |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Capture | [[GTD]] | `10 - Inbox` |
| Organize | [[PARA]] + [[Johnny Decimal]] | `40`–`70` |
| Distill | [[Zettelkasten]] + [[Map of Content (MOC)]] | `30 - Permanent`, `80 - MOC` |
| Express | [[LLM Wiki]] | site / wiki |
