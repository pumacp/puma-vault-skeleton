# PUMA Vault Skeleton

A **generic, reusable, self-documenting knowledge-organization skeleton** for Obsidian (and any Markdown tool). It is built for anyone who must turn complex, abstract, fuzzy information into high-quality structured knowledge — students, teachers, researchers, artists, programmers, information managers, writers, and PhD candidates.

> The vault documents itself **using its own methods**: every explanatory note is classified with Johnny Decimal + PARA, linked through Maps of Content, written as a Zettelkasten note, and processed through the Capture → Organize → Distill → Express cycle.
