# Example Area — Standard

> A generic, topic-neutral example of an **Area** note (PARA). An Area is an ongoing responsibility with a standard to uphold, not a goal to finish. See [[PARA]].

## The standard
Describe the level of quality or upkeep you commit to maintaining in this area (e.g. "inbox processed weekly", "dependencies patched monthly").

## Signals it is healthy
- A short list of indicators you can check at a glance.

## How this note connects
- Upkeep is recorded in [[EX-Area-Review-Log]].
- Indexed by [[MOC Areas]].
- Reviewed during the [[GTD]] weekly review.
